About The Little Joy Project

Comfort, creativity, and support for families facing difficult circumstances.

A youth-led organization focused on bringing comfort, creativity, and support to children and families experiencing hardship.

Our Mission

Helping children and families feel seen, encouraged, and cared for.

The Little Joy Project is a youth-led community organization dedicated to supporting children and families experiencing hardship, crisis, or instability through comfort, creativity, enrichment, and compassionate community care. Beginning with activity kits and growing toward volunteer initiatives, donation drives, enrichment programs, and community partnerships in the Richmond, VA area, we strive to create joyful experiences, provide meaningful support, and help children and families feel seen, encouraged, and cared for during difficult times.

Our Story

Starting with activity kits and growing into a larger community initiative.

The Little Joy Project began with a simple hope: to make children feel supported during stressful or uncertain moments. What started with activity kits is growing into a broader youth-led effort with future goals for donation drives, enrichment programs, volunteer events, and community partnerships in the Richmond, VA area. Each project is designed to offer comfort, spark creativity, and remind families that their community cares.

Our Vision

Joyful experiences can build stronger communities.

To create joyful, comforting, and enriching experiences for children and families facing difficult circumstances and to build stronger, more compassionate communities through youth-led service.
