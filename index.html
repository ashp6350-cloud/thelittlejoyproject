About Us

Small acts of kindness can make a lasting impact.

The Little Joy Project is a youth-led community organization dedicated to bringing comfort, creativity, and support to children and families experiencing hardship. We are beginning with activity kits and growing toward volunteer initiatives, donation drives, enrichment programs, and community partnerships in the Richmond, VA area.

Program Areas

Three ways Little Joy is growing its mission.

☀

Comfort & Care

Activity kits are part of our current work. Birthday boxes, welcome kits, school supplies, and care packages are future ways we hope to help children feel supported.

🎨

Enrichment & Creativity

Future art activities, reading programs, creative workshops, and educational opportunities can encourage children to learn, create, and grow.

✦

Community & Joy

As we grow, we hope to organize volunteer events, community drives, family activity days, and joyful experiences across the Richmond, VA area.
