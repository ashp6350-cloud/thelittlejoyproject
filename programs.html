Program Areas

Comfort, creativity, enrichment, and community care.

The Little Joy Project is beginning with activity kits and growing toward programs focused on comfort, creativity, enrichment, and community care for children and families experiencing hardship in the Richmond, VA area.

☀

Comfort & Care

Current and planned support focused on helping children feel comforted and cared for during stressful situations.

  • Activity Kits
  • Birthday Boxes
  • Welcome Kits
  • Holiday Drives
  • School Supplies
  • Care Packages
🎨

Enrichment & Creativity

Future programs focused on helping children learn, create, and grow despite instability.

  • Art Classes
  • Reading Programs
  • STEM Activities
  • Creative Workshops
  • Educational Kits
  • Tutoring Volunteers
✦

Community & Joy

Future initiatives focused on connection, celebration, and positive experiences.

  • Volunteer Events
  • Community Drives
  • Family Fun Days
  • Celebration Programs
  • Shelter Activity Events
  • School Partnerships
